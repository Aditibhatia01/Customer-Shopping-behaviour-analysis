# Customer-Shopping-behaviour-analysis

📝 Key Description

1. Business Problem
Retailers handle massive amounts of transaction data across various demographics and product categories. Key challenges include:

Understanding which age groups drive the most value.

Identifying the impact of discounts on overall purchase amounts.

Monitoring the conversion rate of subscription-based loyalty programs.

Tracking regional sales performance to optimize inventory.

👉 Without a centralized dashboard, identifying high-value customer segments remains a manual and slow process.

2. Purpose of This Dashboard
To create a single source of truth for shopping behavior and sales performance. It helps stakeholders:

Identify high-performing product categories (Clothing, Footwear, etc.).

Analyze the relationship between subscription status and spending.

Improve marketing ROI by targeting the most active age demographics.

Enable data-driven decisions for discount and shipping strategies.

3. Key Metrics (Top KPIs)

Total Customers: 3.9K

Average Purchase Amount: $59.76

Average Review Rating: 3.75 ⭐

👉 These KPIs provide an immediate health check of the retail operations.

4. Key Visuals & What They Show

🌍 Filters (Left Panel): Subscription status (Yes/No), Gender (Male/Female), Color, and Shipping Type.

Enables dynamic slicing by customer profile and logistics.

📊 Revenue by Category: Funnel chart showing Clothing as the top leader ($104K).

Insight: Marketing should focus on high-volume apparel.

🍩 % of Customer by Subscription: Donut chart showing a 92.7% non-subscriber majority.

Insight: Significant opportunity to grow the subscription base.

📈 Sales by Age Group: Bar charts comparing "Young Adult" vs "Middle-aged" vs "Senior".

Insight: Young Adults (1028 sales) are the most frequent shoppers.

🔍 Key Insights You Can Drive

📌 Clothing Dominance: Clothing is the primary business driver, accounting for nearly 45% of total category revenue.

📌 The Subscription Opportunity: Only 7.3% of customers are currently subscribers. Converting even 5% of the "No" group could drastically stabilize recurring revenue.

📌 Demographic Sweet Spot: The "Young Adult" segment is the most active, but the "Middle-aged" segment follows closely in total spending, indicating high purchasing power in mid-life stages.

📌 Standardized Shipping Preference: By analyzing the shipping slicer, the business can identify if "Express" shipping leads to higher review ratings compared to "Standard."

📌 Rating Benchmarking: An average rating of 3.75 suggests customers are satisfied but not "delighted." Focus is needed on categories with ratings below 3.0 (like specific items in the "Blouse" category seen in the data cleaning phase).

💡 Business Impact / Decisions Enabled

Loyalty Growth: Launch a "Subscriber-Only" discount to target the 92.7% non-subscriber base.

Inventory Management: Increase stock for "Clothing" and "Accessories" while reviewing the "Outerwear" strategy due to lower relative volume.

Targeted Ads: Focus social media ad spend on the "Young Adult" (18-35) demographic as they show the highest engagement levels.

Shipping Optimization: If "Free Shipping" is driving high volume but lower margins, consider a minimum purchase threshold based on the $59.76 average spend.

Summary (One-Liner): 🛒 This dashboard helps retailers optimize category performance, increase subscription loyalty, and target high-value demographics using data-backed insights.
